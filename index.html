Who we are

A Learning Center with a fun, creative and adaptive approach to teach and learn Spanish as a second language.

What We want

We offer families an immersive and flexible Spanish learning program. As well as a multicultural afterschool education in a fun, engaging, and inclusive environment.

Our values

At our core is the idea that differences and diversity make everything better and stronger. As in the animal and natural world, diversity is not only necessary but it creates better communities and environments in which we can flourish, develop and grow both as individuals.

How we work

We offer three programs that care for and educate your children

Seasonal Activities (SA)

Because we want to teach Spanish as much as we can, there is no chance you can miss to be part of MIA, that is why we designed seasonal activities, which allow your children to get involved with a second language.

Out Of School Hours (OOSH)

After-school hours childcare for children until 12 years old.

Spanish Tutoring Class (STC)

Differentiated lessons designed for Spanish as a second language students of any age or ability.
