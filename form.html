Welcome!
We are Multicultural Immersion Academy, a Learning Center with an innovative approach to teaching and learning Spanish as a second language. We use creative pedagogical techniques and believe that the path to acquiring a second language is also the way to genuinely develop essential human skills such as critical thinking, creativity, communication, and collaboration. We offer three programs:

  • Out of School Hours .
  • Private Tutoring Class.
  • Seasonal Activities
